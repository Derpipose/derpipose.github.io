Who are we? Why did we make this?

Tree, my husband, and I, Riley, wanted to use the projects requred for my classes to serve two fold purposes. First, it was a way to fulfill the class project requirements and second, be able to help us out in some way. What this means, is that we wanted a way to have our homebrewed, or cusomized, games of Dungeons and Dragons be just a little easier to navigate. This is a way to keep track of all of the campaigns, and keep all of the resources in one easy to access place. Really, we are just a couple of nerdy loving people that just want to see some fun stories come about.
